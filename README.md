# pm-case-studies
This repository is a collection of problems and pain points I observed, validated and suggested alternative solutions

I come from six years of B2B SaaS implementation and onboarding. I have spent my career on the other side of every PM's blind spot in the room where products land, watching what breaks, understanding why users disengage.
Now I am transitioning into product space. This repository is where I practise product thinking in public.


Each case study follows the same process:
1. Notice a real pain point from personal experience
2. Validate it with real people
3. Research what already exists and why it falls short
4. Design a solution grounded in the problem, not assumptions
